PGP: Nutrition

Enhancing nutrient availability through microbial solubilization.

Biocontrol Agents

Defense mechanisms derived from Antimicrobial BGCs.

  • Lytic Enzymes: Chitinases & Glucanases for pathogen wall degradation.
  • Competition: Siderophores & HCN production.
  • Targeted Action: Bacteriocins & NRPS/PKS logic.

Stress Relief

Helping crops survive drought, salinity, and temperature shock.

  • Hormones: IAA, Cytokinins, Gibberellins.
  • Enzymes: ACC Deaminase for stress reduction.
  • Metabolites: Trehalose synthesis & Proline accumulation.
